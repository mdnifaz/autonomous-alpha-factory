# SPEC-005 Capability Graph Specification

## Purpose
Defines how capabilities are introduced, evaluated, promoted and retired.

## Capability States
Research
Backtest
Simulation
Paper
Limited Deployment
Full Deployment
Retired

## Evaluation Inputs
- Risk adjusted returns
- Stability
- Resource efficiency
- Regime fitness
- Correlation impact

## Dependency Graph
Capabilities may depend on prerequisite capabilities.

## Allocation
Resources and capital are assigned dynamically based on capability scores.

## Governance
All capability transitions are auditable and reversible.
