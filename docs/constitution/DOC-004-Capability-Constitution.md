# DOC-004 Capability Constitution

## Purpose
Defines how the Autonomous Alpha Factory evolves, expands capabilities, allocates capital and retires underperforming capabilities.

## Core Principles
- No hardcoded capital milestones
- No hardcoded promotion thresholds
- No manual capability approvals
- Evidence-based evolution
- Risk-first progression

## Capability Graph
Capabilities are represented as nodes with dependencies.

Examples:
Spot Trading
 -> Futures Trading
 -> Funding Arbitrage
 -> Market Making
 -> Statistical Arbitrage
 -> Multi-Exchange Trading

A capability may only activate when dependency requirements are satisfied.

## Capability Score
Calculated dynamically from:
- Risk-adjusted return
- Sharpe ratio
- Sortino ratio
- Profit factor
- Drawdown stability
- Regime robustness
- Consistency
- Execution quality

No single metric controls promotion.

## Promotion Pipeline
Required stages:
1. Research
2. Backtesting
3. Simulation
4. Paper Trading
5. Shadow Trading
6. Limited Capital Deployment
7. Full Deployment

A capability may not skip stages.

## Adaptive Thresholds
Promotion thresholds are dynamic.

Influenced by:
- Market volatility
- Portfolio risk
- Treasury health
- Current drawdown
- Organizational maturity

## Demotion Rules
Capability may be demoted when:
- Performance deteriorates
- Risk increases
- Regime robustness collapses
- Execution quality degrades

## Retirement Rules
Capability is retired when:
- Long-term negative expectancy
- Repeated failure after retraining
- Structural market change invalidates assumptions

## Capital Allocation
Capital allocation is dynamic.

Inputs:
- Capability score
- Correlation impact
- Risk contribution
- Current market regime
- Treasury objectives

Cash is a valid allocation.

## Capability Governor
Responsibilities:
- Evaluate capability health
- Approve promotions
- Trigger demotions
- Trigger retirements
- Recommend retraining

## Constitutional Constraints
Capabilities may evolve autonomously.

Capabilities may not:
- Modify Risk Constitution
- Modify Kill Switch Rules
- Modify Audit Requirements
- Modify Emergency Procedures
